Spendometer

Spendometer is a powerful expense-tracking app that simplifies financial management for users. With effortless expense entry, intuitive categorization, and comprehensive data visualization through charts and graphs, users gain valuable insights into their spending patterns. The app enables informed decision-making, budgeting, and goal-setting, helping individuals take control of their finances and achieve financial well-being.

SmartAI

SmartAI is an innovative and interactive web-based chatbot interface that utilizes HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to provide real-time user interactions. With a visually appealing design and responsive layout, it offers a seamless user experience across various devices. Leveraging OpenAI's API key, SmartAI demonstrates natural language processing capabilities, enabling it to perform internet searches and deliver accurate, contextually relevant responses to user queries. The incorporation of custom fonts, audio feedback, and distinct message containers enhances engagement, making SmartAI an intelligent, user-friendly, and dynamic platform for meaningful interactions and information retrieval.
